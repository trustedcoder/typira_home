Privacy Policy

Welcome to Typira ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience on our mobile application and related services (collectively, the "Service").

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use the Typira keyboard and mobile application. Please read this policy carefully to understand our practices regarding your data.

To function as an intelligent AI assistant, Typira requires "Full Access" to be enabled in your iOS settings. This is a technical requirement by Apple for custom keyboards to access the internet. We use this permission for exactly two purposes:

  1. AI Processing & Memory: To send the text segments you type to our secure servers. This allows us to provide real-time rewriting suggestions, grammar checks, and build a personalized "Memory" of your context/style to assist you better.
  2. Calendar Integration: To access your device's calendar events only when you explicitly request it (e.g., "Schedule a meeting for tomorrow") to check availability or create events.

Strict Privacy Commitment: We do NOT use "Full Access" to log your passwords or credit card numbers. The iOS system automatically disables Typira in password fields ("Secure Text Entry"), ensuring we never see sensitive credentials.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information to provide better services to all our users. The types of information we collect include:

A. Information You Provide to Us

  • Account Information: When you register for Typira, we collect your name, email address, and password (hashed).
  • User Content (Typing Data): As you use the Typira keyboard, we collect and process the text you type. This data is transmitted to our servers to:
    • Generate real-time AI suggestions and corrections.
    • Build your "Contextual Memory" (e.g., remembering your tone, project details, or frequent topics) to personalize future suggestions.
  • Calendar Data: If you use scheduling features, we access event details (title, time, attendees) to perform the requested action.

B. Information We Collect Automatically

  • Usage Data: We collect anonymous information about how you use the app, such as features used, frequency of use, and crash reports.
  • Device Information: We may collect information about your mobile device, including device model, operating system version, and unique device identifiers.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • Service Delivery: To operate the keyboard, generate AI responses, and manage your account.
  • Personalization: To remember your writing style and context (via the "Memory" feature) so the AI becomes more helpful over time.
  • Improvement: To analyze usage trends and improve the accuracy of our AI models.
  • Communication: To send you updates, security alerts, and support messages.

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal data. We disclose your information only in the following robustly controlled circumstances:

  • Service Providers: We may share data with trusted third-party vendors (e.g., cloud hosting providers, AI processing engines) who assist us in operating the Service. These partners are bound by strict confidentiality agreements.
  • Legal Compliance: We may disclose information if required by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities (e.g., a court or a government agency).

4. Data Retention & Control

We retain your personal information and User Content only for as long as is necessary for the purposes set out in this Privacy Policy.

  • Account Data: Retained as long as your account is active.
  • AI Memory: Retained to provide personalization. You have full control over this data:
    • Clear AI Memory: You can purge your entire AI history (typing history, style context, and learned metadata) at any time via the Settings > Clear AI Memory option in the app. This resets your AI profile while keeping your account active.
    • Account Deletion: You can delete your entire account and all associated data at any time via Settings > Account Deletion.

5. Data Security

We use administrative, technical, and physical security measures to help protect your personal information. Your data is encrypted during transmission (using SSL/TLS) and when stored on our servers. However, please be aware that no electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure.

6. Your Data Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access: The right to request copies of your personal data.
  • Correction: The right to request that we correct any information you believe is inaccurate.
  • Deletion: The right to request that we erase your personal data ("Right to be Forgotten").
  • Restriction: The right to request that we restrict the processing of your personal data.

To exercise these rights, please contact us at support@typira.com.

7. Children's Privacy

Our Service is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. If we discover that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will immediately delete it.
